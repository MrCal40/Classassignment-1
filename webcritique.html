Webcritique

www.footlocker.com

6 things I like about www.footlocker.com

  • Their website has most things you will like all on the main page.
  • I like the shoe image brings you to a section of the exact shoe with a breakdown in prices.
  • I like the simple coloring nothing over dramatic with all kind of colors that take away from the shoes.
  • The website is well put together their logo does not over power the products they are advertising.
  • Besides the shoe images being clickable so are the logos which I think is a pretty neat feature.
  • I like the fact the chat bubble follow up as you scroll up and down the website.

4 things www.footlocker.com can improve.

  • The navigation towards the top when you click on a topic is not as fluent as I would like, it makes page it difficult to get back to the main page.
  • I would like to have all the images loaded into different tabs.
  • There is too much information at the bottom of the page, perhaps condensing some of the information into one.
  • When you click on one of the top topics (new arrivals, men’s, women’s, kids’) there are too many selections.
